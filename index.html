About Tata Steel

Our Legacy

Founded in 1907, Tata Steel is among the top global steel companies with an annual crude steel capacity of 34 million tonnes per annum. We are the world's most geographically diversified steel producer, with operations in 26 countries and commercial presence in over 50 countries.

Our Vision

To be the global steel industry benchmark for value creation and corporate citizenship through the excellence of our people, our innovative approach and our overall conduct.

Our Commitment

Tata Steel is committed to reducing its carbon footprint and has set ambitious targets to achieve net-zero carbon emissions by 2045. Our sustainability initiatives include water conservation, renewable energy adoption, and circular economy practices.
